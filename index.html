Pokemon Team Evaluator - Early Generations

Generation 1

This is a team builder for Red, Blue, and Yellow.

Use the controls below to select your desired sprites, and if you want to see the final evolutions only. Once the sprites load, click on one to add to your team, or double click to remove sprite.

All fields are required. Glitches or other hacks are not taken into consideration.
